## AI-Assisted CTI Workflow Experience

My AI work is framed as analyst decision support, not an analyst replacement. AI-assisted workflows in the portfolio focus on structured enrichment, candidate mapping, summarization, and review support.

Guardrails:

- AI output is untrusted until analyst-reviewed.
- Candidate scoring requires analyst validation.
- Candidate ATT&CK mapping requires evidence review.
- Shared tooling does not prove actor identity.
- CTI output should identify evidence, gaps, assumptions, confidence, and operational use.
